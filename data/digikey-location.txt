Where is DigiKey Located?
DigiKey's headquarters and product distribution center is in Thief River Falls, Minnesota, USA. DigiKey is an authorized distributor whose core product is stored, packed, and shipped from our world-class facility in the United States. Regional support centers are in Israel, Germany, the Netherlands, China, India, Hong Kong, South Korea, and Japan.

In 2019, DigiKey began a Marketplace program that is an online platform for an extended list of manufacturers and distributors to sell products through DigiKey. Marketplace items ship directly from the Supplier.
