Recruiting Privacy Notice
DATE OF LAST MODIFICATION
December 13, 2018
Scope
DigiKey and its group companies ("DigiKey" or "we") are committed to safeguarding your privacy.

This Recruiting Privacy Notice ("Privacy Notice") describes DigiKey’s collection, use, disclosure, transfer, and storage ("Processing") of DigiKey applicants' / prospective candidates' personal data as part of the recruitment process. How we process your personal data may vary by jurisdiction based on applicable law.

We recommend you read this Recruiting Privacy Notice in full to ensure you are fully informed. For additional information about how we process your personal information in connection with your general use of DigiKey websites and our use of cookies and similar technologies outside the scope of this Privacy Notice please see our Privacy Policy and Cookie Policy.

Personal Data We Collect

We will collect and process any personal information that you provide to us in connection with your application and recruitment process with us. This may include:

contact information, such as name, email address, physical addresses, and telephone number in order to communicate with you directly in advance of or during an on-site or phone interview;
information that you provide to us when submitting a job resume (CV) whether via registration in the Applicant Self-Service Center (United States only), by email, or at a recruiting event;
any other information that you provide to us in connection with your job application including, academic and professional qualifications (degrees, titles, skills, language proficiencies, training information) employment background/history or any other employment related-information.
As part of the application process we may request additional information for purposes described at the time it is collected or as necessary to establish, administer and manage the recruitment process. DigiKey does not require you to provide any of the following data as part of the recruitment process but may ask for the following information to be provided on a voluntary disclosure basis when required or allowed by law: racial or ethnic origin, religious or philosophical beliefs, biometric data, data concerning health or gender, and criminal history.

DigiKey collects information about you in connection with its recruitment processes in a variety of ways, including directly from you, but also from third party or public sources. For example, personal information might be collected via the application process on our Applicant Self-Service Center. Resumes (or CVs) may be collected at interviews or during other forms of assessment during the application evaluation process. We may also review public records information in order to evaluate your qualifications, or conduct background screenings relevant for the job to which you applied, as permitted by applicable law. We may also verify personal information from business directories such as LinkedIn.

We may also collect information from third parties, such as references supplied by former employers, or recruiting agencies that supply DigiKey with potential job applications in relation to evaluating your eligibility for employment.

If you are in the United States and choose to apply for a position at DigiKey, we will request that you create an account with a username and password on our Application Self-Service Center. You will also have the option to "Apply with LinkedIn". By using this feature, you will allow LinkedIn to share your information with DigiKey. You may also submit your resume or CV via email, if made available in the Application Self-Service Center. If outside the U.S., you can submit your CV or resume using the "Submit Resume" button which will send an email to the relevant Human Resources department.

How We Use and Retain Your Personal Information

DigiKey processes your personal information to administer recruitment activities, such as using your information to contact you about potential job opportunities and to take necessary steps prior to entering into an employment relationship.

As part of the recruitment process, this may involve processing your personal information in the following ways and for the following purposes:

establishing, administering, and managing all aspects of the employment application process (including travel arrangements and expenses for interviews);
where necessary to comply with applicable legal or regulatory requirements;
review and verification of your resume and application related information;
evaluating and reporting the demographic makeup of our applicants where allowed by law (e.g., diversity reporting);
communicating with you about the status of your application and/or other opportunities; and,
other purposes described to you at the time we collect your personal information.
The personal information collected will be stored in accordance with applicable laws and kept as long as needed to carry out the purposes described in this Privacy Notice or as otherwise required by applicable law. Generally, this means your personal information will be retained until the end of your employment application review period plus a reasonable period of time thereafter to respond to application-related inquiries, to consider you for other immediate roles, to deal with any legal matters or, as applicable. We may retain your personal information for longer where you have requested to be considered for other roles.

If your application for a position at DigiKey is successful, the personal information collected during the recruitment process will be added to your human resources file and retained for the duration of your employment with us and in accordance with our retention policy.

Disclosure

As part of the recruitment process, DigiKey may disclose your personal information to the following categories of recipients:

internally to DigiKey related companies, DigiKey personnel, contractors, and vendors who require such information to assist the relevant DigiKey company with whom you have applied, with establishing, administering, and managing the recruiting process such as members of the HR and recruitment team, interviewers involved in the recruitment process, managers in the business area with a vacancy and IT staff if access to the data is necessary for the performance of their roles;
third-party service providers who process personal information as data processors on behalf of DigiKey in connection with their provision of certain services to us. For example: in the United States, we may use Employment Screening Associates (background checks), Global Safety Network (pre-employment drug tests) and eSkill (pre-employment skill testing). In Germany, we may use Liebwein GmbH Recruiting (employment agency), the Official Munich Job Center (employment agency) and Stepstone.de (recruiting agency).
third parties with whom you instruct DigiKey to share your personal information;
any law enforcement, regulatory or other government entities, court or other authorized third parties where DigiKey has a good faith belief such disclosure is required to comply with applicable law or to respond to a valid legal process;
other third parties to act on a good faith belief that disclosure is necessary to protect your, or other third parties’ vital interests or safety; and
other corporate entities (e.g. an actual or potential buyer, or its agents and advisers) if DigiKey goes through a business transition, such as a merger, acquisition by another company, or sale of all or a portion of its assets.
Third parties processing personal information (such as background or employment check service providers) on DigiKey’s behalf (i.e. our data processors) must abide by data privacy and security requirements and may only process DigiKey company personal information as instructed by DigiKey.

Tracking Technologies

When you visit DigiKey’s recruiting web pages or the recruiting portions of our site, DigiKey may observe your activities, interactions, preferences, transactional information, and other computer and connection information (such as IP address) relating to your use of our websites and our services.

We or our service providers may use cookies and other technologies similar to cookies; such as, web beacons, tags, and scripts. We use these technologies to authenticate your access to our recruiting site. For information about the specific cookies (or similar technologies) that we use on this website and how you can block or turn off cookies, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

Data Transfers

Your personal information may be transferred to, and processed in, countries other than the country in which you are resident. These countries may have data protection laws that are different to the laws of your country and, in some cases, may not be as protective.

Specifically, our website servers are located in the USA, and our group companies and third-party service providers and partners operate around the world. This means that when we collect your personal information we may process it in any of the countries we operate in.

However, we have taken appropriate safeguards to require that your personal information will remain protected in accordance with this Privacy Notice. These include implementing the European Commission’s Standard Contractual Clauses where appropriate, which can be provided on request. We have implemented similar appropriate safeguards with our third-party service providers and partners and further details can also be provided upon request.

Children

Our recruiting and career sites are not intended to solicit, collect or process personal information from any individual under the age of sixteen [16]. If you are a parent or guardian, please contact us using the contact information beneath "Contact" section below, if you believe information regarding a child has been collected by us through any of our recruiting or career sites.

Legal Basis for Processing and Privacy Rights (EEA candidates only)

If you are an applicant or candidate in the European Economic Area, our legal basis for collecting and using the personal information described above will depend on the personal information concerned and the specific context in which we collect it.

However, we will normally only collect personal information from you only where we have your consent to do so, where we need the personal information to enter into a contract with you (i.e. enter into an employment or work relationship with us), or where the processing is in our legitimate interests and not overridden by your data protection interests or fundamental rights and freedoms. In some cases, we may also have a legal obligation to collect personal information from you or may otherwise need the personal information to protect your vital interests or those of another person.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

You have the right to request access, rectification or erasure of your personal information.
You have the right to ask us to restrict processing, to object to our processing in certain circumstances and request portability of your personal information in certain circumstances.
If you have provided consent for the processing of your personal data you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time which will not affect the lawfulness of the processing before your consent was withdrawn.
You also have the right to lodge a complaint to the competent data protection supervisory authority if you believe that we have not complied with the requirements of the GDPR with regard to your personal information.
You can exercise these rights by contacting us using the contact details provided under "Contact" section below. We respond to all requests we receive from individuals wishing to exercise their data protection rights in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

For the purposes of European data protection laws, the Digi–Key company that you are applying for a position with will be the data controller of your information. In addition, DigiKey will also act as a joint controller and a processor of your data in respect of DigiKey's recruitment processing activities.

Security

DigiKey has implemented appropriate technical measures designed to protect your personal information against accidental or unlawful destruction or accidental loss, damage, alteration or industry standard security measures to ensure the security of information. However, the security of information transmitted through the Internet can never be guaranteed.

DigiKey is not responsible for any interception or interruption of any communications through the Internet or for changes to or losses of information. Users of our sites are responsible for maintaining the security of any password, user ID, or other form of authentication involved in obtaining access to password protected or secure areas of any DigiKey websites.

In order to protect you and your information, DigiKey may suspend your use of a website, without notice, pending an investigation, if any breach of security is suspected. Access to and use of password protected and/or secure area of any DigiKey’s recruiting web pages is restricted to authorized users only.

Access

We take steps to help ensure that the personal information we maintain is accurate and up to date, and that you have the ability to access and make corrections. If you need to update, change or request access to your information, please log into your account on our Applicant Self-Service Center (United States only) or send an email to the applicable Contact noted below.

Choice

You are under no statutory or contractual obligation to provide personal information to DigiKey during the recruitment process and you may choose not to provide some of the personal information for the purposes set out in this Privacy Notice. Depending on the information you may choose not to provide, we may not be able to process your application, request or onboard for employment related purposes.

Updates to this Privacy Notice
