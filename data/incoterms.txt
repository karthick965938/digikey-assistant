What are the Incoterms for my shipment?
INR Currency

When placing an order in INR Currency Incoterms are CPT (Duty, customs, and taxes due at time of delivery).

USD Currency

Incoterms for UPS, FedEx or DHL freight pre-paid shipments are CPT (Duty, customs, and VAT due at time of delivery).

Note: Incoterms will be shown on the Preview-page of your online order and will vary depending on your shipping location, VAT validity and company/personal status.
