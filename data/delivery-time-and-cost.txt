What is the delivery time and cost?
Most packages to India arrive in 4 days. This is the transit time from our distribution center in the United States, and customs clearance may impact the time it takes to receive your package.

INR Currency

Free delivery on orders to India of ₹7,000 or more.
A delivery charge of ₹1,200 will be billed on orders less than ₹7,000.
USD Currency

Free delivery on orders to India of $100 USD or more.
A delivery charge of $20 USD will be billed on orders less than $100 USD.
*Marketplace products are not included in DigiKey's free shipping calculations as they ship separately from the vendor.
