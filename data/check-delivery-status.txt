How do I track delivery status and shipping notification?
Once the order has been processed and shipped successfully, you will receive a shipping notification email with tracking information.

Registered myDIGIKEY users

Once your order ships you will receive a shipping notification email with tracking information.
Log in to your myDIGIKEY account
Select Status and History (your most recent orders will be listed at the top of the page)
Select the appropriate invoice to view the shipping information
Guest users

Once your order ships you will receive a shipping notification email with tracking information.
Go to the Order Status page to track your order and review shipments.
